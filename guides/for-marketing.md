# VAST for Marketing Leaders

## What this means for Marketing

Marketing's AI integration is becoming load-bearing — content generation, personalization, audience segmentation, campaign optimization. VAST gives this integration a structural framework instead of feature-by-feature adoption.

## What changes

**Your capability framework gets named.** Marketing Architecture = what AI skills exist (content variant generator, audience segmenter, brand-voice enforcer, claim verifier, A/B analyzer), how they compose (segment → generate → validate → publish → measure), what invariants hold (brand consistency, factual accuracy, regulatory compliance).

**Brand and compliance become invariants, not afterthoughts.** In VAST, invariants are first-class architectural decisions. "Every AI-generated claim passes verification against approved sources" is an invariant — it doesn't get deprioritized when timelines are tight.

## What doesn't change

- Marketing still owns brand, campaigns, and growth
- Your existing processes and tools continue
- No new approvals or governance layers
