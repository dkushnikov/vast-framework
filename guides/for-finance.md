# VAST for Finance Leaders

## What this means for Finance

Finance Architecture = the capability portfolio framework: what financial capabilities exist (variance analysis, forecasting, audit trail, budget modeling), how they compose (data collection → analysis → recommendation → decision support), what invariants hold (audit traceability, regulatory compliance, materiality thresholds).

## What changes

**Audit and compliance become architectural invariants.** Not "we should also make sure it's auditable" — audibility is a first-class architectural constraint. Every AI-assisted financial recommendation has provenance tracing by design, not by afterthought.

**Decision support gets structured.** "Provide forward-looking decision support within 24 hours of any strategic question" = Vision. The capability framework that makes this possible = Architecture. Which strategic questions to prioritize = Strategy. Specific analyses = Tactics.

## What doesn't change

- Finance still owns financial governance and controls
- Existing audit processes continue
- No new tools or reporting layers required
